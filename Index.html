Who am i?

My name is Jasper van den Heuvel, I'm a 20 year old ICT & Media design student at Fontys university of applied sciences in Eindhoven which is where i also live! i started with the ICT & Media design study in 2022. before this i studied application and media developer at Summa College, from 2019 until 2022! I really enjoyed this study and it made clear for me that i like the webdevlopment side a lot! Thats is one of the main reasons why i chose Media design. Besides school i also work at Eindhoven Airport as Flow coordinator, i started working at Eindhoven Airport in the summer of 2022 as host beceause of the long lines, and at the end of 2022 i got promoted :)
