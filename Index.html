Development

What is it?
Proofs of concepts (pocs): programming small pocs to focus on basic skills. Integrating various smaller pocs into a larger application. The focus is a fundamental understanding of JavaScript.

Validated requirements: to be determined by the bachelor student by doing research (end users, stakeholders). For the AD students: given in advance in the instructions of exercises and assignments.

Version control tools: a tool (e.g. GitHub, GitLab) to monitor your progress and backup your work (push, pull, commit).

Research

What is it?
Exploratory research: research aimed to gain familiarity with a phenomenon, to gain experience and to acquire new, significant personal insights into a given situation. Exploratory research is flexible and can address research questions of all types: what, why and how.

Methods: use and apply methods from the DOT framework and the CMD methods pack.

Curious and critical attitude: you have the ability to be amazed and you are open to wonder. You like to try out new things, you want to access new knowledge and you feel the urge to gain an understanding of your findings. You do not take facts or results for granted, but you verify your insights by checking multiple sources and engaging in dialogue with peers and experts.
